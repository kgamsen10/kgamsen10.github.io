Winter Themed Cereal

Cereal Box // Poster

Explore The Season

Prompt

With the provided logo, develop a winter-themed Cheerios packaging design that thoughtfully incorporates diverse artistic styles while effectively emphasizing the essence and visual identity of the winter season.

Solution

I selectively incorporated the rubber hose style into key elements of the overall composition, rather than applying it uniformly across the entire design. This approach allowed me to achieve a balanced integration of the new aesthetic while maintaining the integrity and clarity of the core visual concept.
