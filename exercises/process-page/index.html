Winter Themed Cereal

Cereal Box // Poster

Explore The Season

Prompt

With the provided logo, develop a winter-themed Cheerios packaging design that thoughtfully incorporates diverse artistic styles while effectively emphasizing the essence and visual identity of the winter season.

Solution

I selectively incorporated the rubber hose style into key elements of the overall composition, rather than applying ituniformly across the entire design. This approach allowed meto achieve a balanced integration of the new aesthetic while maintaining the integrity and clarity of the core visual concept.

Research & Analysis

I gathered and analyzed a range of rubber hose–style visual references to accurately develop both the bowl and snowflake character. In parallel, I explored seasonal color palettes to ensure the design effectively conveys a cohesiveand visually compelling winter setting .   I conducted targeted research on rubber hose–style anatomy and characteristic angles, these references shaped the overall composition and viewing perspective of the packaging. For example, the bowl element is oriented to the right, and I aligned the composition accordingly to maintain stylistic consistency. This directional choice also influenced the color palette, ensuring a cohesive and visually harmonious design.

Ideation

The design was enhanced by more clearly integrating the defining characteristics of the rubber hose style to strengthen its visual identity. Element separation was improved through the use of subtle drop shadows and refined stroke treatments, creating greater clarity and depth within the composition. The logo was scaled up to reinforce brand presence and establish a stronger visual hierarchy. Additionally, the illustration was further developed while maintaining consistency with the winter theme and overall stylistic direction. Finally, alignment and spacing issues were addressed to ensure a more balanced, cohesive, and polished final design.
