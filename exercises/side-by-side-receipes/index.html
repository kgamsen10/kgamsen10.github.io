CRISPY BONELESS CHICKEN THIGHS

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time:15 minutes Total Time:30 minutes Yields:4

If you've been sleeping on skin-on chicken thighs, this is your wake up call. A flour and cornstarch dredge seasoned with smoked paprika, lemon pepper, and Worcestershire gives you the crunchiest crust with the juiciest inside — all done in a shallow pan in about 30 minutes. These are the chicken thighs that'll change the chicken game!
